Love

Shiv and Parvati’s love is timeless and a symbol of devotion, respect, and eternal love. Their love transcends time and is not bound by the physical world. It is a divine bond that remains unbroken through time and space.
They are the perfect example of a loving and devoted couple whose love is not limited to the mortal world but extends beyond it. These quotes capture the essence of the love between Shiv and Parvati and the power of their divine bond.

Ganapathi Bappa

Ganesh Chaturthi is a festival celebrated specially by Hindus. The festival usually falls in the month of August or September. It is the day on which Lord Ganesha the youngest son of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvathi was born. Ganesha is considered the God of Wisdom and Knowledge. He is also referred to as Mangal Murthi, the symbol of prosperity, happiness and welfare. As Lord Ganesha brings in positivity every auspicious work begins after worshipping him. People Chat “ Ganapathi Bappa Morya, Mangal Murti Morya” to make their deity happy.
